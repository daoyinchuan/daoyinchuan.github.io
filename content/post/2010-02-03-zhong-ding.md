This is a VERY interesting observation from a book I am currently reading.

"Central equilibrium, in turn, is primarily a mental/spiritual function.  A physical manifestation of _zhong ding_ is a perfectly balanced body.  A perfectly balanced body, with the _qi_ sunk to the _dantian_, is achieved only by a calm and peaceful mind."  

> _(Yang Yang, [Taijiquan: The Art of Nurturing, The Science of Power](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0974099007/gardendigest), 2005, pg. 30)_
