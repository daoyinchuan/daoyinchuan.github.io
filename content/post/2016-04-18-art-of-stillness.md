Just finished reading a small book name [The Art of Stillness: Adventures in Going Nowhere (TED Books)](http://www.amazon.com/The-Art-Stillness-Adventures-Nowhere/dp/1476784728) and throughly enjoyed it.  You can also see his TED talk [here](https://www.ted.com/talks/pico_iyer_the_art_of_stillness?language=en). The way he writes and talks is extremely calming.  Those who are seeking more happiness and enjoyment in life can benefit from embracing stillness and making a choice to go "nowhere".
