One of my favorite quotes from **Cheng Man-Ching** is

>"There are no secrets, they are just so small we overlook them."

This seems to be true not just for Tai-chi-chuan practice but for all of life.  Awareness seems to be so focused that we miss the small but *VERY* important points.
