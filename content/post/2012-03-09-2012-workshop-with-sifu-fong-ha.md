We will be holding the Daoyin Chuan 2012 Workshop with [Sifu Fong Ha](http://www.fongha.com/) on August 17th and 18th.  Friday (17th) night will be from 7:30pm to 10pm and Saturday (18th) will be from 9:30am to 5pm.  We will go to a local restaurant for our lunch break so be sure to bring money or a brown bag.  Either way we will be eating lunch together.  The location for the workshop is at [The Grateful Pose](http://www.thegratefulpose.com/) (Yoga Center) at [845 N 400 E, Bountiful, UT](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=845+N+400+E,+Bountiful,+UT&hl=en&sll=37.0625,-95.677068&sspn=57.641503,99.052734&t=h&hnear=845+N+400+E,+Bountiful,+Utah+84010&z=17).

The cost will be $110.00 for the workshop,_ if paid in advance_.  Friday night at the door will $30.00 and Saturday at the door will be $95.00.  Attached is a registration form with more details: [2012 Workshop with Sifu Fong Ha](http://daoyinchuan.files.wordpress.com/2012/03/2012-workshop-with-sifu-fong-ha.pdf).

There will be a recording of the workshop that will be available in September/October time-frame for a minimal cost.  This years workshop will be a great learning experience in a very nice and peaceful environment.  Looking forward to seeing you there.
