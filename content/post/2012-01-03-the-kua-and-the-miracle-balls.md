If you have a tight Kua you might want to look into the miracle ball method as way to loosen and open it up more. I have found it very beneficial in helping with my hip displasia making my Yiquan and Taijiquan more effective. Its principles are in complete alignment with the internal arts as taught by Ha Sifu.
