One thing learned was I need to take a few minutes to prepare for the meditation.  If I don’t there are too many interruptions which definitely interfere with entering stillness.  Today, Thursday, I entered into deep quietness while doing the meditation while standing.  In that state it was much easier to feel the natural movement of energy within the body.
