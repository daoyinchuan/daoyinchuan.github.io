2012 Workshop with Sifu Fong Ha

Fri, Mar 9, 2012

We will be holding the Daoyin Chuan 2012 Workshop with Sifu Fong Ha on August 17th and 18th.  Friday (17th) night will be from 7:30pm to 10pm and Saturday (18th) will be from 9:30am to 5pm.  We will go to a local restaurant for our lunch break so be sure to bring money or a brown bag.  Either way we will be eating lunch together.  The location for the workshop is at The Grateful Pose (Yoga Center) at 845 N 400 E, Bountiful, UT.

The cost will be $110.00 for the workshop, if paid in advance.  Friday night at the door will $30.00 and Saturday at the door will be $95.00.  Attached is a registration form with more details: 2012 Workshop with Sifu Fong Ha.

There will be a recording of the workshop that will be available in September/October time-frame for a minimal cost.  This years workshop will be a great learning experience in a very nice and peaceful environment.  Looking forward to seeing you there.
