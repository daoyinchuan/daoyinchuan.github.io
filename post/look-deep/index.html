Look Deep

Thu, Dec 8, 2011

It is wise for us to look deep into ourselves, beyond the mere reflection that we see. To be able to tap into our inner regions is to find a treasure greater than that which could ever come from the ground. Meditation is a useful tool to do this. To clear the mind allows us to see things and ourselves as we really are. To find ourselves is to find the answer to perhaps the greatest of all questions. Even if we do not find the answer, merely to ask the question can be enough.

Roshi Hogan’s Teachings Of The Tao by Roshi Hogan
